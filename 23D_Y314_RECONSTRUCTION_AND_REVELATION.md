# Y314 Reconstruction, Fracture, and Revelation

## Purpose

This file owns the Y314 campaign layer: the year when the living try to
rebuild law, trade, and local order after the Deepholm campaign and the
Long Dark, only to discover that recovery is now inseparable from the
barrow crisis.

Boundary rule:

- 23C owns the late Y313 Long Dark collapse and dread convergence
- 23D owns Y314 reconstruction, faction fracture, and supernatural
  revelation
- 23E owns the Y315 Iron Barrow climax and aftermath that grows from the
  state
established here

## 1. Prompt 15 — rebuild Y314 as reconstruction, fracture, and revelation

Y314 should not be written as a full collapse year from the first page.
Instead, it must begin as a serious human attempt to recover.

People count the dead, settle winter debts, reopen routes, argue over food,
repair docks, sign patrol contracts, and test whether law can still
function. That recovery effort matters because it gives the next fracture
real weight.

The deeper truth becomes undeniable only because competent people keep
pushing into it:

- Wardens gather evidence rather than rumors
- Sigrun tries to rebuild trade and political legitimacy
- Gest keeps the ledger honest enough to show what the land can actually
  bear
- Voss keeps the band working instead of drifting into despair
- Thorne and Dalla become unwilling proof that the old buried system is
  waking

### Y314 doctrine

- reconstruction must feel real, partial, and worth attempting
- political recovery should create new leverage fights, not neat peace
- supernatural revelation should arrive through evidence, ritual effect,
  and
repeated confirmation rather than a single lore dump
- every improvement in stability should expose a deeper structural weakness
- the Galdr-Forge line should move from suspicion to credible fact by year
  end

### Recovery and fracture streams

| Stream                        | What it looks like in play                                                        | Why it matters                                             |
| ----------------------------- | --------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | ---------------------------------------------------------- |
| civic recovery                | dead counts, planting attempts, trade fairs, patrol contracts, and local bargains | shows that the human world is still trying to function     |
| faction realignment           | Compact growth, Iron Grip shrinkage, Widow debt webs, hired bands changing sides  | the post-war map is redrawn by survival logic              |
| evidence of the deeper threat | breached barrows, Dalla's visions, measurable Veil thinning, rune fragments       | the truth becomes undeniable through accumulating proof    |
| moral strain                  | bargains for medicine, paid debts, refugee burden, blood-price fallout            | personal arcs remain attached to the larger year structure |

## 2. Main Y314 objects

### O314-R01 — Counting the Surviving World

- **Year window:** Y314 spring, days 1-45
- **Class:** reconstruction object
- **POV:** Gest, Voss, Petra, or a settlement-facing witness
- **Canon anchors:** EVT_314_S01_001, EVT_314_S03_003, EVT_314_S05_005,
EVT_314_S06_006, EVT_314_S09_009, EVT_314_S12_012, EVT_314_S15_015
- **Setup:** the thaw reveals what the previous year truly cost, and every
  power
in the Rimevegr starts rebuilding from scarcity rather than victory
- **Decision pressure:** what gets restored first when law, food, trade,
  and
morale all need the same scarce labor?
- **Competent options:** repair essential routes, pay honest debts, secure
  short
patrol work, protect refugees, and accept limited recovery goals
- **Desperate options:** stage hollow feasts, overpromise harvests, hide
  body
counts, or starve smaller settlements to prop up prestige centers
- **Likely state touches:** food_security, settlement_trust, band_solvency,
refugee_pressure, compact_legitimacy
- **Routes:**
  - **O314-R02** — spring rebuilding feeds directly into the summer law
    theater.
  - **O314-R03** — early quiet in the barrows becomes evidence, not relief.
  - **O314-R05** — weak recovery determines how much winter the region can
    bear.
- **Mystery-box note:** answers how the land begins to recover, but not why
  the
silence in the barrow fields feels more dangerous than noise

- **Mystery-box note:** answers that the buried system is real, but not yet

  whether
the coalition forming under pressure will hold when the truth becomes

  across

  still
delays the final truth of what waits under the Iron Barrow itself

## 3. Usage rule for Y314 drafting

Use this file whenever the campaign is dealing with attempted recovery,
post-war political rearrangement, barrow emergency coordination, and the
steady conversion of buried lore into confirmed strategic truth.

- 23C ends with the Long Dark inheritance of fear and fracture
- 23D begins with reconstruction and ends with undeniable revelation
- 23E should build forward from the command, debt, and truth state
  established
here
