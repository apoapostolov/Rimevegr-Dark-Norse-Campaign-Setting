## [2026-06-16] — v1.0.0

### Added

- Initial public release of the **Rimevegr: Dark Norse Campaign Setting** — a
  complete, ready-to-run world bible for a dark, low-fantasy Norse mercenary
  campaign.
- Full setting bible (`01_RIMEVEGR_SETTING_BIBLE.md`) documenting the world's
  tone, climate, society, and magic system with grounded early-medieval
  authenticity.
- Complete simulation ruleset (`20_SIMULATION_RULES.md`) covering combat,
  travel, logistics, morale, economy, recruitment, and band management.
- Living-world data files: settlement economy, bestiary, contracts, weather,
  calendar, political state, and NPC tracking under `data/`.
- Python simulation engine (`scripts/`) for combat resolution, logistics,
  weather, morale, economy, NPC generation, and campaign journaling.
- Campaign content: five campaign arcs spanning Y311–Y315
  (`23_CAMPAIGN_ARCS_AND_PLOT_SEEDS.md`), 41 narrative vignettes
  (`24_VIGNETTES_AND_SCENES.md`), barrow dungeons, and faction profiles.
- Player-facing roleplaying prompt (`00_ROLEPLAYING_PROMPT.md`) and novel
  writing mode (`00_NOVEL_WRITING_PROMPT.md`).
- Interactive hex map tool (`tools/RIMEVEGR_HEX_MAP/`) for geographic
  exploration.
- Cover art and logo in `images/` for campaign presentation.
