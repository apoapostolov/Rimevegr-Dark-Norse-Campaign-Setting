# Bestiary Image Prompts

## Animal

### Brown Bear (ANI_BEAR_01)

Tier 3 | predator

A massive brown bear rearing on hind legs in a forest clearing, forepaws
spread wide. Dark matted fur with burrs. Mouth open showing yellowed
teeth. Dappled forest light, birch trees behind. Ground torn up from
previous digging. Late summer, warm tones.

### Cave Bear (ANI_BEAR_02)

Tier 4 | predator

An enormous bear filling a cave entrance, skull impossibly broad,
scarred from old fights. Bones scattered on the cave floor — elk
antlers, smaller skulls. Eyes reflecting torchlight from deep sockets.
Cave walls glistening with moisture. Darkness behind the shape. Heavy
breath visible in cold air.

### Raven Swarm (ANI_BIRD_01)

Tier 1 | bird

A rising cloud of ravens lifting off a muddy battlefield strewn with
corpses. Dozens of black shapes against a grey sky. One raven in the
foreground on a broken spear shaft, beak bloody, staring directly at
the viewer. Overcast, muted palette, wet ground.

### Eagle (ANI_BIRD_02)

Tier 1 | bird

A white-tailed eagle mid-dive against a mountain backdrop, wings folded,
talons extended forward. Massive wingspan partially visible. Rocky cliff
face behind with a stick nest. Blue sky with high clouds. Sharp detail
on feathers and talons. Sense of speed and power.

### Avalanche (ANI_HAZ_01)

Tier 4 | hazard

A massive avalanche cascading down a steep mountain slope, white wall
of snow engulfing dark pine trees. Tiny figures on a mountain path
below, caught mid-stride. Debris of broken timber in the snow cloud.
Bright winter light, blue shadows on the snow above. Sense of
overwhelming scale and speed.

### Bog-Sink (ANI_HAZ_02)

Tier 2 | hazard

A figure sinking waist-deep in dark bog water, arms outstretched,
sphagnum moss and sedge grass around. The surface looks deceptively
solid nearby. Dark peaty water. Mist low over the bog, distant
silhouettes of scrubby trees. Muted earth-tone palette, grey
overcast sky. Desperation on the figure's face.

### Ice-Crack (ANI_HAZ_03)

Tier 3 | hazard

A frozen lake surface with a jagged dark crack running across it, black
water visible below. A figure mid-fall as ice tilts, arms windmilling.
White ice, grey sky, distant snow-covered shore. Steam rising from the
exposed water. Sharp diagonal composition, sense of sudden catastrophe.

### Rockfall (ANI_HAZ_04)

Tier 2 | hazard

billowing. A figure pressing against the cliff wall with a shield
raised overhead. Shattered stone debris on the path. Steep canyon

Dust-choked air.

### Flash Flood (ANI_HAZ_05)

Tier 3 | hazard

uprooted trees and debris. A camp in the foreground being engulfed,
tent canvas and loose timber spinning in the current. Figures
scrambling uphill. Heavy rain, dark clouds. Violent spray where water
hits rock walls. Raw power of water.

### Whiteout Blizzard (ANI_HAZ_06)

Tier 3 | hazard

leaning into horizontal wind, cloaks snapping. Snow so thick the
background is pure white. One figure reaching blindly ahead. Ground
and sky indistinguishable. Tiny detail of a rope between the two
figures. Monochrome white and grey, brutal wind direction visible
in the snow streaks.

## Supernatural

### Seidr-Corrupted Wolf (SUP_CORR_01)

Tier 2 | seidr_corrupted

A wolf half again larger than natural, standing at the edge of

spaced. No breath visible despite the cold. Absolutely silent. Snow

### Seidr-Corrupted Bear (SUP_CORR_02)
