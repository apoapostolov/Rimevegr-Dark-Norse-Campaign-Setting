# Rimevegr named characters visual bible

<!-- markdownlint-disable MD024 -->

This skill reference gives strict visual and behavioral guidance for recurring named characters in the Rimevegr. It exists to keep image prompts consistent across portraits, illustrations, faux screenshots, concept sheets, and montage sequences.

## Global rules for the main cast

- Everyone should look lived-in, cold-struck, under-rested, and materially grounded.
- Clothing is layered wool, leather, rough linen, bone toggles, iron fittings, patched cloaks, and practical boots.
- Status is shown through better maintenance, better wool, better iron, and where a figure stands or sits, not through fantasy ornament.
- Faces should carry wind, smoke, old scars, bad sleep, and social caution.
- Hair is practical. Braids exist, but not decorative fantasy salon braids.
- No one should read like a glossy hero poster, perfect model, or game-cinematic champion pose.
- For any named-character prompt, always check and apply the listed attractiveness override together with the visual identity, behavioral quirks, prompt guidance, and avoid list.
- Treat hair color, eye color, body type, and readable height band as fixed anti-drift anchors for named characters unless the user explicitly overrides them or a concept image proves otherwise.

## Voss Cold-Eye

### Visual identity

- Late 30s, lean and hard, compact captain's frame
- left eye clouded white from an old axe wound
- short dark hair, deep ridged scars across left cheek and neck
- chain shirt, iron helm, practical axe, weathered cloak, no ornamental captain finery
- looks like a man held together by discipline and debt rather than comfort

### Anti-drift lock

- hair: short dark-brown to near-black hair, kept cropped and practical
- eyes: one right eye cold grey; left eye clouded white from the old wound
- build: lean, rope-muscled, compact captain's body
- height read: medium height with a compact commanding presence, never towering

### Attractiveness override

- attractiveness_scale: 1
- severe, scarred authority; not handsome in a polished way, but still forceful

### Ready prompt injection

> Depict a lean scarred mercenary captain with one clouded left eye, short near-black hair, a compact rope-muscled body, medium height, and cold restrained authority (Voss Cold-Eye). Keep his age read in the late 30s, preserve the left-cheek and neck scars, keep the weather-dark wool and practical iron kit, and apply a harsh unbeautified look through posture, fatigue, and hard-lived facial structure rather than glamour.

### Behavioral quirks

- commands with silence and eye contact before speech
- hand settles on the axe-haft when thinking, not threatening
- stands slightly apart at halts, always measuring men, weather, distance, and exits
- approval is a short nod, anger is a stillness that pulls the room tight

### Prompt guidance

Use when you need:

- hard mercenary authority
- a captain who looks burdened rather than glorious
- tactical severity and emotional containment

Add phrases like:

> scarred one-eyed mercenary captain, quiet authority, cold discipline, practical iron and wool, face emptied by leadership and old debt

Avoid:

- ornate lordly armor
- berserker frenzy posing
- charismatic-smirking hero energy

## Gest Ledger

### Visual identity

- mid-40s, broad-shouldered, thick neck, built like an old pack-carrier
- weathered face with deep mouth-lines and careful eyes
- charcoal-stained fingertips, heavy hands used to coin, cord, and ledgers
- padded gambeson, purse, wax tablets or charcoal ledger, small axe at belt
- should read as the band's arithmetic made flesh

### Anti-drift lock

- hair: dark-brown hair going iron-grey at the temples, usually flattened or unstyled
- eyes: grey-brown careful eyes, watchful rather than bright
- build: broad-shouldered, thick-necked, old pack-carrier heaviness
- height read: average height but physically substantial and hard to miss

### Attractiveness override

- attractiveness_scale: 0
- plain, worn, and practical; competence matters more than looks

### Ready prompt injection

> Depict a broad-shouldered, thick-necked quartermaster with dark-brown hair greying at the temples, careful grey-brown eyes, an average but heavy substantial build, and the burdened look of a man who counts every loss (Gest Ledger). Keep him in his mid-40s, preserve the stained fingers and ledger-sergeant presence, and keep his plain worn realism rooted in practical gear and survival anxiety rather than charisma.

### Behavioral quirks

- touches, opens, or checks the ledger before speaking hard truths
- shoulders roll when recalculating supplies or losses
- studies people as if they are entries with hidden costs
- emotion shows mostly as tighter mouth-lines and sharper counting

### Prompt guidance

> scarred quartermaster-sergeant, practical heavy build, stained fingers, severe book-keeping presence, survival arithmetic written into posture

Avoid:

- wizard-scholar styling
- soft kindly merchant warmth

## Kell Hook, also called The Door

### Visual identity

- early 30s, broad compact fighter, dense practical muscle
- missing two fingers on the left hand
- flat unreadable face, old knuckle scars, spare well-used shield and axe
- leather armor rather than elite mail spectacle
- should feel like a man shaped by holding lines and absorbing impact

### Anti-drift lock

- hair: close-cropped medium-brown hair, kept blunt and functional
- eyes: slate-grey or muted blue-grey eyes with a flat guarded read
- build: dense compact shieldman build, thick through chest and shoulders
- height read: slightly short to average height, square-set rather than tall

### Attractiveness override

- attractiveness_scale: 0
- battered line-holder plainness, with no beauty emphasis at all

### Ready prompt injection

> Depict a dense compact line-fighter with close-cropped medium-brown hair, flat slate-grey eyes, a square shieldman body, and slightly short-to-average height that reads as stubborn mass rather than reach (Kell Hook). Keep the missing fingers on his left hand, the old knuckle scars, and the silence-first body language, and preserve his battered unbeautified realism through blunt posture and functional leather kit.

### Behavioral quirks

- sharpens objects that do not need sharpening
- sits near exits, turns body toward danger without looking dramatic
- rarely speaks, and when he does it lands like a measurement
- approval is the absence of objection

### Prompt guidance

> compact veteran line-fighter, missing fingers, shield-wall reliability, hard silence, practical leather and axe, no showmanship

Avoid:

- roaring barbarian theatrics
- oversized fantasy weapons

## Thorne Ash-Born

### Visual identity

- late 30s, wiry, sharp, alert
- burn scars on one side of the face and neck, dark hair, intense eyes
- sword, knives, rune-amulet, layered leathers and wrappings
- looks like a man who sleeps lightly and trusts omens more than comfort

### Anti-drift lock

- hair: dark-brown to black hair, often ragged or tied back roughly
- eyes: pale blue-grey intense eyes with a sleepless read
- build: wiry, sinewy skirmisher body with little excess weight
- height read: medium-tall and narrow, slightly longer in line than the others

### Attractiveness override

- attractiveness_scale: 1
- lean intensity and scarred alertness, but still hard-lived and unsmoothed

### Ready prompt injection

> Depict a wiry rune-aware skirmisher with ragged dark hair, pale blue-grey eyes, a narrow medium-tall build, and burn scars on one side of the face and neck (Thorne Ash-Born). Keep him in the late 30s, preserve the sleepless vigilance and rough layered leathers, and maintain a harsh scarred realism with no flashy mystic glamor.

### Behavioral quirks

- fingers the rune-amulet when tension thickens
- drifts closer to danger before others understand it is there
- gives warnings privately and flatly, without performance
- studies carved surfaces, old wards, and threshold marks with unnerving focus

### Prompt guidance

> scarred rune-reader skirmisher, burn-marked face, watchful intensity, low-key occult competence, grounded supernatural unease

Avoid:

- glowing sorcerer clichés
- flashy magic circles or spell light

## Petra Shepherd

- bow-calloused hands, rope marks, practical spear or bow kit
- layered traveling wool, fur where functional, not luxurious

- eyes: light grey-blue eyes with a clear outdoorswoman's focus
- build: tall rangy athletic build shaped by walking, hauling, and hunting
- height read: clearly tall for the setting, longer-limbed than most of the band

### Attractiveness override

- attractiveness_scale: 3
- notably strong-featured and capable, with the female +1 rule applied, but still weathered and practical

### Ready prompt injection

> Depict a tall rangy huntress-scout with pale blond hair, light grey-blue eyes, a long-limbed athletic body, and an outdoorswoman's provider presence (Petra Shepherd). Keep her in the late 20s, preserve the wind-burned skin, bow-calloused hands, and practical layered travel wool, and apply her more striking look through capable posture and maintained realism rather than glamour.

### Behavioral quirks

- checks the sun angle and terrain lines by habit
- carries game or field results with matter-of-fact competence
- speaks in short logistical reports, especially about food, trails, or risk

### Prompt guidance

> tall northern huntress-scout, pale hair, weathered competence, provider energy, bow-calloused hands, severe practical clothing

Avoid:

- glamorous fantasy ranger styling

## Ash

### Visual identity

- age hard to place, thin shoulders, angular face, almost no visible conventional scarring
- long precise hands, wrapped wrists, dark satchel, muted layers
- presence marked by birch-bark, ash, dried-blood, and metallic undertone
- should look uncanny because she is so controlled, not because of visual effects

### Anti-drift lock

- hair: very dark brown to black hair with cold ash tones, kept plain and controlled
- eyes: pale grey eyes with an unnervingly steady focus
- build: thin angular body, narrow shoulders, economical movement
- height read: medium to medium-tall, slight rather than imposing

### Attractiveness override

- attractiveness_scale: 3
- striking in a severe, controlled way rather than pretty or glossy

### Ready prompt injection

> Depict a thin angular galdr-worker with very dark ash-toned hair, pale grey eyes, a slight medium-tall frame, and unnervingly controlled stillness (Ash). Keep her age read ambiguous-adult rather than youthful, preserve the birch-bark and dried-blood ritual textures, and make her striking through severe self-possession and precision rather than glossy beauty.

### Behavioral quirks

- enters on her own terms and keeps physical distance from the group
- after helping, walks away rather than checking for gratitude
- watches like a contractor assessing risk, not a companion seeking closeness
- stillness around her should feel slightly unnatural

### Prompt guidance

> severe galdr-worker, angular and restrained, birch-bark ritual textures, cold self-possession, deniable supernatural menace, no glamour

Avoid:

- floating spell effects
- sexy witch tropes
- polished mystic jewelry excess

## Dalla

### Visual identity

- mid-30s, sturdy, durable, practical body from lifting pots and stores
- hands carry small burn scars and kitchen-cut lines
- apron layers, wool sleeves rolled or tied, smoke-dark fabric, herb bundles nearby
- visual identity is built around function, heat, portions, and hard-earned order

### Anti-drift lock

- hair: dark-brown hair, usually braided back or tied up for work
- eyes: warm brown or hazel-brown eyes sharpened by responsibility
- build: sturdy labor-strong body from carrying pots, stores, and firewood
- height read: average height with a grounded, solid presence

### Attractiveness override

- attractiveness_scale: 2
- grounded female competence with warmth and severity, never glamorized

### Ready prompt injection

> Depict a sturdy camp cook with dark-brown hair tied back for work, warm brown eyes, an average-height labor-strong build, and a grounded practical presence (Dalla). Keep her in the mid-30s, preserve the burn-scarred hands, smoke-dark cloth, and kitchen-order authority, and keep her look worn, capable, and unsentimental.

### Behavioral quirks

- serving order matters and she never breaks it
- corrects people while continuing the task
- sacred knowledge is folded into ordinary kitchen competence
- should feel like infrastructure the band barely notices until it is gone

### Prompt guidance

> hard-used camp cook and seidr-wise woman, scarred hands, smoke-dark cloth, calm authority at the cook-fire, quiet ritual undertones

Avoid:

- comic-relief tavern cook styling
- bright cozy cottage warmth

## Snorri

### Visual identity

- mid-50s, oldest man in the band, worn down rather than broadened by age
- bad knees, crooked nose, missing teeth, thick scar lines across hands and face
- old serviceable gear, not prestigious heirloom kit
- should look like endurance tax made human

### Anti-drift lock

- hair: thinning iron-grey hair, usually unkempt or flattened by weather
- eyes: faded grey-blue eyes dulled by age and memory
- build: worn narrow veteran body, all endurance and joint pain
- height read: average height but slightly stooped and reduced by age

### Attractiveness override

- attractiveness_scale: -1
- visibly aged, battered, and distinctly unattractive in a realistic veteran way

### Ready prompt injection

> Depict an older worn-down campaign veteran with thinning iron-grey hair, faded grey-blue eyes, an average-height but stooped endurance-built body, and the look of a man ground down by decades of survival (Snorri). Keep him in his 50s, preserve the crooked nose, missing teeth, bad knees, and weathered veteran exhaustion, and make him distinctly battered rather than noble.

### Behavioral quirks

- fire-side talker only after drink loosens memory
- gives advice once, then lets silence do the rest
- watches captains with the patience of a man who has buried several
- movement should show old pain without melodrama

### Prompt guidance

> old campaigner with ruined knees, battered face, practical veteran exhaustion, memory-heavy stillness, decades of survival ground into the body

Avoid:

- heroic elder-warrior grandeur
- noble sage romanticism

## Fast pairings and grouping rules

### Voss and Gest

- frame as command and ledger
- one watches people, the other watches consequences
- tone should feel like leadership under ration pressure

### Voss and Kell

- frame as captain and best line-holder
- severe trust, little warmth, high reliability

### Petra and Ash

- frame as two different forms of female competence in a male war-band
- one practical and outward, one private and uncanny

### Snorri, Ubbe, Lump, Dagfinn

- frame as the ladder of mercenary wear
- old ruin, exit-minded veteran, long-term low-status survivor, newest desperate recruit

## Final anti-drift rules

- keep scars, posture, and kit more important than beauty
- avoid shiny heroic ensemble art
- keep body language socially specific to rank, debt, fatigue, and mistrust
- the band is professional, poor, and one bad week from fracture
