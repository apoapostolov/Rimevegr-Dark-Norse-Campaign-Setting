# Rimevegr faction leaders and rivals visual bible

<!-- markdownlint-disable MD024 -->

This skill reference covers the visual identity and behavior of major regional powers, rival mercenary bands, and important dead or history-heavy figures. Use it to keep factions distinct from one another instead of letting every armed group collapse into the same generic Viking look.

## Global faction rules

- Every band should feel materially plausible and socially specific.
- Rivalry is shown through maintenance level, posture, group spacing, insignia habits, animal remains, banner roughness, and camp order.
- The Rimevegr does not do polished heraldry. Marks are crude, repeated, local, and often half-faded.
- Leaders are dangerous because of judgment, leverage, and reputation, not because of fantasy-crown spectacle.
- Female leaders can sit about +1 higher on the attractiveness scale than comparable men if the image needs sharper presence or maintained authority, but they must still stay severe, historical, and unsentimental.

## Voss's Black Axes

### Core band identity

- restrained, practical, mercenary-professional
- mixed gear quality but consistently maintained
- scarred competence, tired discipline, survival-first kit
- no uniform, but a clear shared seriousness

### Anti-drift faction lock

- palette: soot-black, iron-grey, wet-wool brown, and muted weather blues
- silhouettes: practical shields, plain axes, patched cloaks, travel packs, and no theatrical trophies
- group spacing: disciplined, alert, and tired rather than swaggering or feral
- power read: hired professionals under debt and ration pressure, not berserk raiders

### Ready prompt injection

> Depict Voss's Black Axes as a restrained professional mercenary band in worn but maintained northern kit: patched wool, practical iron, scarred faces, disciplined spacing, and survival-first seriousness. Keep them materially grounded, visibly tired, and competent under pressure, with no glossy hero posing, no fantasy uniformity, and no theatrical barbarian excess.

### Mood words

- hired steel
- cold trust
- ration pressure
- durable hierarchy

## Rival mercenary bands

## The Three Wolves

### Visual identity

- leaner, meaner, more openly predatory than Voss's band
- wolf pelts, tooth cords, rough trophies, harder eyes
- aggressive spacing and prowling camp posture

### Anti-drift faction lock

- palette: dirty greys, old blood browns, wolf-hide tans, and smoke-black leather
- signature markers: tooth cords, pelt scraps, and mean pack silhouettes
- group read: intimidation-first, hungry, invasive, and visibly cruel
- avoid drift into polished elite guards or comic berserker costumes

### Ready prompt injection

> Depict the Three Wolves as lean predatory rival mercenaries with wolf-hide markers, tooth cords, hard hungry faces, and prowling pack spacing. Keep the gear practical and brutal rather than ornate, and preserve the sense that intimidation is part of their trade.

### Behavioral feel

- intimidation is part of the job even when not needed
- they want witnesses to remember the threat

### Prompt addon

> predatory rival mercenaries with wolf-hide markers, cruel confidence, hungry pack spacing, practical brutality without fantasy ornament

## The Bone Pack

### Visual identity

- bone charms, scavenged pieces, white-scratched shields, death-haunted roughness
- equipment looks assembled from aftermath and bad country

### Anti-drift faction lock

- palette: bone-white scratches, grave dirt, hide brown, and weathered iron
- signature markers: scavenged bones, stripped kit, and famine-country ugliness
- group read: half-forager, half-scavenger, fully human and deeply unsettling
- avoid cartoon necromancer styling or supernatural glow effects

### Ready prompt injection

> Depict the Bone Pack as grave-haunted mercenary scavengers with bone-marked gear, stripped-survival equipment, white-scratched shields, and a bad-country starvation look. Keep them human, rough, and death-familiar without turning them into fantasy undead.

### Behavioral feel

- their camp reads as half-foragers, half-ghouls, but still human
- too comfortable around graves, remains, and stripped bodies

### Prompt addon

> grave-haunted mercenary scavengers, bone-marked gear, scavenged survival kit, hard famine-country ugliness

## The Silent Oar

### Visual identity

- coastal raider minimalism, wet rope, tar-black wood, seal-hide, salt-stiff cloaks
- muted dark palette, fewer words, more watching

### Anti-drift faction lock

- palette: tar-black, deep salt-grey, wet wood brown, and cold sea blue
- signature markers: rope, hooked tools, seal-hide, and salt-burned cloth
- group read: quiet timing, silent coordination, black-water professionalism
- avoid loud pirate clichés or decorative naval fantasy styling

### Ready prompt injection

> Depict the Silent Oar as quiet coastal mercenaries and raiders in tar-dark gear, rope-worn kit, salt-stiff cloaks, and weathered seaborne faces. Keep their menace rooted in muteness, efficiency, and black-water discipline rather than noise or spectacle.

### Behavioral feel

- quiet group timing, efficient boarding and shore movement
- menace through silence rather than shouting

### Prompt addon

> silent coastal mercenaries and raiders, tar-dark gear, salt-weathered faces, disciplined muteness, black-water professionalism

## The Hollow Hall

### Visual identity

- more iron, more camp structure, harder military discipline
- grim mining-road textures, slag-black and rust-orange accents
- helmets and shields maintained in matching practical standards

### Anti-drift faction lock

- palette: slag-black, rust-orange, cold iron-grey, and soot-dark cloth
- signature markers: matching maintenance standards, harder geometry, and industrial grime
- group read: bureaucratic brutality and militarized order
- avoid noble-knight polish or fantasy legion spectacle

### Ready prompt injection

> Depict the Hollow Hall as a harsh iron-road mercenary company with disciplined camp geometry, soot-dark maintenance, matching shields, and industrial frontier grime. Keep the tone bureaucratic, severe, and militarized rather than noble or glamorous.

### Behavioral feel

- camp order matters, brutality is bureaucratic
- should feel close to Ordovast's expanding military machine

### Prompt addon

> harsh iron-road mercenary company, disciplined camp geometry, industrial grime, militarized frontier realism

## The Iron Tide Remnant

### Visual identity

- old sea-war survivors, patched marine gear, good weapons carried by tired bodies
- barnacled memory, storm-worn faces, relic quality without glamour

### Anti-drift faction lock

- palette: storm-grey, sea-black, faded sailcloth off-white, and rust-salt brown
- signature markers: patched marine gear, old professional weapons, veteran fatigue
- group read: bitter endurance and old pride under visible attrition
- avoid triumphant naval-hero glamor or clean admiral styling

### Ready prompt injection

> Depict the Iron Tide Remnant as storm-worn veteran seafarers turned mercenaries with patched marine kit, good old weapons, tired bodies, and bitter enduring pride. Keep them weather-beaten, haunted by former status, and fully stripped of glory.

### Behavioral feel

- veteran bitterness, fewer illusions, harder competence
- more haunted by what they used to be than by what they are now

### Prompt addon

> storm-worn veteran seafarers turned mercenaries, old professional pride under attrition, worn marine kit, bitter endurance

## The Grave Wardens

### Visual identity

- anti-barrow specialists, ash, iron stakes, rope, ward marks, grave tools
- solemn practical blacks and greys, not priestly finery

### Anti-drift faction lock

- palette: ash-grey, grave-black, iron, chalk white, and rope brown
- signature markers: ward stakes, grave tools, anti-dead procedure kit
- group read: solemn, procedural, and exhausted by necessary horror
- avoid priestly pomp, occult glamour, or theatrical exorcist costume design

### Ready prompt injection

> Depict the Grave Wardens as grave-specialist field operatives with iron stakes, ash marks, rope, and practical anti-barrow tools. Keep them procedural, tired, and exact, with grounded dread rather than priestly theater.

### Behavioral feel

- procedural, grim, tired, purposeful
- look like people who know exactly how much the dead can cost

### Prompt addon

> grave-warden specialists with iron stakes and ash marks, practical anti-undead field kit, solemn procedural dread

## The Red Tide

### Visual identity

- blood-rust and sea-filth accents, rough trophies, more chaos than discipline
- equipment looks stolen, mixed, and half-repaired

### Anti-drift faction lock

- palette: blood-rust, bilge-dark brown, dirty red cloth, and sea-filth black
- signature markers: stolen mixed gear, rough trophies, unstable posture, and appetite for violence
- group read: fast cruelty, poor discipline, and raider volatility
- avoid comic madness, chaos-cult styling, or clean military symmetry

### Ready prompt injection

> Depict the Red Tide as savage coastal raiders with blood-rust cloth accents, stolen mixed kit, rough trophies, and unstable violent energy. Keep them grounded, brutal, and half-repaired rather than mythic or stylized.

### Behavioral feel

- fast violence, cruelty, unstable morale, raider appetite
- feel like a coastal fever breaking inland

### Prompt addon

> savage red-tide raiders, blood-rust cloth accents, stolen mixed kit, unstable violent energy, grounded cruelty

## Major regional leaders

## Jarl Hrothgar of Frostfjord

### Visual identity

- late 40s to early 50s, sea-cold fjord ruler with well-kept practical wealth
- trimmed beard, watchful blue-grey eyes, heavy wool and restrained silver details
- should read as profitable honesty rather than generosity

### Anti-drift lock

- hair: iron-blond to weather-grey hair, kept orderly rather than vain
- eyes: cold blue-grey evaluating eyes
- build: broad mature ruler's frame, solid rather than soft
- height read: medium-tall with seated authority and transactional calm
- status markers: better wool, dry boots, maintained silver, and deliberate seat placement

### Attractiveness override

- attractiveness_scale: 2
- notable maintenance and authority, but still stern, wind-cut, and historically grounded

### Ready prompt injection

> Depict a sea-cold fjord jarl with iron-blond weathering, blue-grey calculating eyes, a broad mature build, and practical high-status wool with restrained silver (Jarl Hrothgar of Frostfjord). Keep him transactional, composed, and prosperous without softness or lordly fantasy excess.

Prompt cue:

> profitable fjord jarl, controlled authority, good wool and silver kept practical, coastal cold calculation

## Warchief Ordovast the Iron-Grip

### Visual identity

- brutal expansionist, military pressure in human form
- heavier escort, iron-rich scene language, harder contrast, less domestic warmth
- a man who wants submission to look efficient

### Anti-drift lock

- hair: dark hair going iron-grey, cropped for military order
- eyes: hard steel-grey eyes with no softness
- build: thick heavy war-chief frame, more force than grace
- height read: tall or tall-feeling through escort pressure and physical mass
- status markers: iron-heavy gear, harder camp order, and intimidation through structure

### Attractiveness override

- attractiveness_scale: 1
- forceful and formidable, but never polished or charismatic in a glossy way

### Ready prompt injection

> Depict a heavy militarized war-chief with cropped dark-iron hair, hard steel-grey eyes, a thick powerful frame, and iron-rich authority built on discipline and expansion (Warchief Ordovast the Iron-Grip). Keep the read efficient, brutal, and unsentimental rather than theatrical.

Prompt cue:

> iron-fisted war-chief, expansionist northern strongman, militarized authority, grim industrial frontier power

## The Pale Widow of Ashen Reach

### Visual identity

- cold, clever, recently widowed, politically venomous without melodrama
- ash-toned clothing, controlled wealth, severe face, no theatrical mourning excess
- should feel like a household ruler weaponized into strategy

### Anti-drift lock

- hair: pale ash-brown or dark blond hair kept severe and controlled
- eyes: pale grey watchful eyes, dangerous through restraint
- build: narrow composed upper-class frame, disciplined rather than delicate
- height read: average to slightly tall, commanding through stillness
- status markers: ash-toned fine wool, controlled mourning dress, and impeccably maintained austerity

### Attractiveness override

- attractiveness_scale: 3
- striking, severe, and intelligent-looking without any glossy court-beauty drift

### Ready prompt injection

> Depict a severe widowed power-broker with ash-toned hair, pale grey assessing eyes, a disciplined narrow frame, and meticulously controlled mourning dress (The Pale Widow of Ashen Reach). Keep her beauty cold, political, and unsentimental, rooted in restraint rather than glamour.

Prompt cue:

> pale widow power-broker, ash-toned authority, measured grief turned political intelligence, dangerous calm

## Jarl Sigrun of Deepholm

### Visual identity

- wealthy, pragmatic, premium but still mountain-practical
- better materials, archive-rich or mine-rich setting cues, no softness
- reads as competence paid for by a harder economy

### Anti-drift lock

- hair: dark blond or chestnut hair kept pinned or braided for order
- eyes: clear grey or stone-blue eyes with a measured read
- build: strong mountain-country frame, maintained and durable
- height read: average to tall, upright and stone-backed
- status markers: good cloak quality, better leather, mine-wealth maintenance, and archive competence

### Attractiveness override

- attractiveness_scale: 3
- notable authority and maintenance with a severe practical edge, never decorative softness

### Ready prompt injection

> Depict a pragmatic mountain jarl with well-kept dark-blond hair, clear stone-grey eyes, a durable upright frame, and wealth shown through maintenance, cloth quality, and stone-backed confidence (Jarl Sigrun of Deepholm). Keep her refined but severe, never ornamental.

Prompt cue:

> pragmatic mountain jarl, refined but severe, wealth shown through maintenance and stone-backed confidence

## Jarl Ulf Vargson of Vargheim

### Visual identity

- traditionalist, wolf-fixated, suspicious of outsiders
- forest authority, pelt markers, hound or wolf presence, blunt old custom energy
- should feel like a man who trusts territory more than diplomacy

### Anti-drift lock

- hair: dark hair with weather-grey streaks, worn in old customary fashion
- eyes: pale watchful eyes, suspicious and territorial
- build: rangy forest-hardened frame, still dangerous with age
- height read: medium-tall with rooted territorial posture
- status markers: wolf or hound markers, forest pelts, and old-custom severity

### Attractiveness override

- attractiveness_scale: 1
- rugged and forceful, but more territorial than handsome

### Ready prompt injection

> Depict a suspicious forest jarl with dark weathered hair, pale territorial eyes, a rangy hard-used frame, and old wolf-warden markers worked into practical northern dress (Jarl Ulf Vargson of Vargheim). Keep him severe, customary, and rooted in territory rather than diplomacy.

Prompt cue:

> forest jarl with wolf-warden identity, old custom severity, guarded territorial authority

## Jarl Helga of Thornwall

### Visual identity

- shrewd, protective, hard-bargaining moor ruler
- wind-cut face, good practical wool, shepherd-country toughness, no ornamental softness
- authority through endurance and care for routes and flocks

### Anti-drift lock

- hair: wind-dark blond or brown hair, tied back for function
- eyes: sharp grey-blue eyes that weigh cost and risk quickly
- build: tall or long-framed moor-country endurance build
- height read: medium-tall with durable shepherd-country presence
- status markers: better wool, route-awareness, and a protector's severe calm

### Attractiveness override

- attractiveness_scale: 3
- strong-featured and commanding with maintained authority, but still weathered and practical

### Ready prompt injection

> Depict a hard-bargaining moor jarl with wind-cut features, tied-back dark-blond hair, sharp grey-blue eyes, and a durable long-framed shepherd-country build (Jarl Helga of Thornwall). Keep her protective, severe, and materially grounded, with authority shown through endurance rather than ornament.

Prompt cue:

> hard-bargaining moor jarl, protective and severe, wool-route authority, practical female rule under attrition

## Settlement authorities and lore figures

## Thane Egil Raven-Eye

- mountain sentinel ruler, stone-wall vigilance, raven association without cartoon symbolism
- should look like a cliff watch made human

## Harbour-Master Inga

- salt-weathered dock authority, practical rope-and-ledger control, nothing wasted
- stance says berth access, repair priority, and coastal leverage

## Elder Brosa

- moor elder, isolated toughness, suspicious stewardship of half-dying land
- should carry the hush of forbidden standing stones

## Reeve Torsten

- fair-dealing village reeve, unglamorous dependable authority, keys and local judgment
- should look like a man trapped between neutrality and bigger predators

## Ferryman Olaf

- river-bottleneck pragmatist, poor in silver but rich in leverage
- quiet toll-keeper energy, wet wood, mud, and watchfulness

## Lore-Keeper Audun

- severe archive custodian, memory as power, cliffside scholarship with political edge
- more careful than mystical, more keeper than priest

## Hermit Ragnhild

- ice-edge seidr authority, blood-price diviner, old northern severity
- should feel like the cold accepted a human bargain and left her standing

Prompt cue:

> ice-reach hermit seer, severe winter face, blood-price divination, permanent veil-edge pressure, grounded dread

## Important dead and historical figures

## The Barrow-King, Jarl Halvdan

### Visual identity

- dead three centuries yet still enthroned
- pale blue burning eyes, motion slow and sovereign, ancient sword across the knees
- grave-rich textiles and corroded authority, not skeleton spectacle

### Behavioral feel

- commands obedience as if time never moved on
- not rabid, not feral, terrifying because it is patient and still believes it rules

### Prompt addon

> ancient enthroned draugr-jarl, pale corpse-light eyes, dead sovereignty, stone throne and grave regalia, cold command rather than frenzy

## The Draugr-Jarl beneath Frostfjord

### Visual identity

- older and uglier than a normal draugr, bound with iron stakes, cold radiating outward
- chamber frost, corpse-pale rot, sealed-horror atmosphere

### Behavioral feel

- existential rather than theatrical
- the sense that this thing should never stand up

### Prompt addon

> bound ancient draugr-jarl under a settlement hall, iron stakes failing, lethal cold radiating from old death, severe subterranean dread

## Final anti-sameness rules

- each faction should have a distinct relationship to maintenance, cruelty, silence, and camp order
- use different terrain anchors for each group instead of recycling the same hill-and-fog background
- leaders should project leverage and consequence more than glamour or battle rage
