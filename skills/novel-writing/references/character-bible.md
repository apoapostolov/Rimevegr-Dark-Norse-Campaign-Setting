## Dossier Template (Writing Use)

```text
### [Call-Name] — [Role]

**Build:** [one physical sentence]
**Hands:** [one occupational sentence]

**Gesture inventory:**
- [habit under calm conditions]
- [habit under pressure]
- [habit visible only to close observers]

**Voice sample:**
- [line 1]
- [line 2]

**Relationship pressure points:**
- [name]: [friction or alignment]
- [name]: [friction or alignment]

**Arc position:** [current beat]
**Trigger:** [what breaks alignment]
**Last significant action:** [scene reference]
```

---

## Drafting Rules

1. Introduce character through action, not profile paragraph.
2. Reuse gesture inventory consistently.
3. Keep dialogue shorter than exposition around it.
4. Let relationship changes appear through proximity and task
   assignment.
5. When a character exits, show redistribution of labor and gear,
   not explicit emotional explanation.

---

## Continuity Checks

- One stable voice fingerprint per recurring character.
- One active arc beat per character per chapter.
- No contradictory wound/function behavior in adjacent chapters.
- Status shifts must appear in action before they appear in summary.

---

## Update Protocol

When canon changes, update `17_CHARACTER_BIBLE.md` first.
This file should only change if the writing process changes.
