# Religious Register and Divine Ambiguity — Writing Rules

This reference is intentionally **craft-only**.

Canonical divine lore has been moved to core docs:

- `07_PANTHEON_AND_DIVINE_PRACTICES.md`
- `01_RIMEVEGR_SETTING_BIBLE.md` (religion and cosmology sections)

Use this file only for prose behavior and constraints.

---

## Core Writing Rules

1. Treat divine language as social reflex, not exposition.
2. Keep invocation usage character-specific and stable.
3. Preserve ambiguity: outcomes may look supernatural, but never
   become explicit proof.
4. Show belief through behavior, not sermons.
5. Keep divine references brief under pressure scenes.

---

## Scene-Level Checks

- No direct god speech.
- No miracle-resolution beats.
- No cosmology infodumps in dialogue.
- No thesis statements about "what faith means."
- If a scene references the sacred, end on consequence, not theory.

---

## Continuity Protocol

When writing or revising:

1. Pull canonical terms and domains from
   `07_PANTHEON_AND_DIVINE_PRACTICES.md`.
2. Validate cultural framing in `01_RIMEVEGR_SETTING_BIBLE.md`.
3. Apply these prose constraints during final pass.
