## Character State Tracking

- **Condition:** Healthy / Exhausted / Wounded (severity + type + location + sublocation) / Frostbitten / Infected (stage) / Incapacitated / Scarred but functional / Dying / Dead
- **Active Wounds:** Tracked per wound record schema (20_SIMULATION_RULES.md § 5.4). Each wound is a discrete entry with ID, location, severity, healing stage, and treatment state.
- **Scars:** Permanent records generated when wounds heal (§ 5.16 wound_scar). Include visibility, narrative tag, and any permanent mechanical effect.
- **Special Conditions:** Wound-Addled (§ 5.15), Iron Scar (§ 5.15), Weather-sensitive, Chronic Pain.
- **Psychological Profile:** Tracked per trauma record schema (20_SIMULATION_RULES.md § 5.20). Each member carries TSP, active/ resolved conditions, behavioral baseline, and coping method. Profiles update after traumatic events, recovery periods, and significant band milestones.
- **Loyalty:** 1-5 (Named Men only; drops when Trigger ignored)
- **Inventory:** Track weight (carry limit = MIG × 5 + 10 kg)
- **Update Frequency:** After every major event (march, combat, pay ritual, contract completion, rest). Wounds update daily during active healing.
