# Named Men and Archetypes

**Project:** Iron Ledger -- Mercenaries of the Rimevegr
**Purpose:** Band archetypes, Named Men templates, and character
interaction rules. All stats use the Iron Ledger system
(see 20_SIMULATION_RULES.md).

---

## 1. Band Archetypes

All bands are called **Svarthird** ("Black Hird") by outsiders.

### Rural Mob ("Fjord Folk" or "Hall-Burned")

Farmers, fishermen, and woodcutters who lost everything.
**Strengths:** Excellent foragers; treat one extra terrain as favored.
**Weaknesses:** Morale checks harder when far from home waters.
**Breaks when:** The captain starts acting like a jarl.

### Tyrant Hird ("Iron Fist")

Built around one brutal captain who rules by fear.
**Strengths:** Use Intimidate for internal discipline. Commons do not
check Morale in first round of combat.
**Weaknesses:** When captain is absent or weak, Morale drops and Named
Men re-evaluate.
**Breaks when:** The captain loses badly in front of the men.

### Military Hird ("Old Huscarls")

Veterans of a jarl's household guard. They drill and hold formation.
**Strengths:** Drill time halved. Commons hold formation without Morale
check. Professional standards.
**Weaknesses:** Pay expectations strict. Single late payment = harder
checks. They question reckless orders.
**Breaks when:** Captain shows ignorance of shield-wall work or orders
pointless atrocities.

### Kin-Clan ("Blood-Shield")

All members share blood, marriage, incest, or sworn shield-oath.
**Strengths:** Morale checks easier when defending kin. Internal
grievances handled by tradition.
**Weaknesses:** Atrocity against shared kin = automatic Morale -2.
Blood obligations pull members away.
**Breaks when:** Captain violates the clan's internal code.

## 2. Named Men Templates

Each Named Man has:

- An earned **call-name** (never self-chosen).
- A **Trigger** -- the thing that breaks loyalty if ignored.
- An **Agenda** -- a personal goal older than the band.
- **Loyalty 1-5** (see 20_SIMULATION_RULES.md for scale).

### Voss "Cold-Eye" -- Captain

Lean, scarred, face like chipped flint. Left eye clouded from an old axe
wound. Speaks only when necessary.
**Stats:** MIG 7, NIM 6, TOU 6, WIT 6, WIL 7, WYR 2
**Skills:** Axes 3, Shields 2, Command 3, Intimidate 2, Navigate 2,
Track 2, Sagas 1, Wyrd-reading 1
**Trigger:** Being given an order that endangers the band for no gain.
**Agenda:** Repay the blood-debt owed to the family of a shield-brother
who died under his command three winters ago.
**Loyalty:** N/A (Captain)

### Gest "Ledger" -- Sergeant / Quartermaster

Thick-necked, broad-shouldered, heavy hands. Always carries the charcoal
ledger. Face like weathered granite.
**Stats:** MIG 6, NIM 4, TOU 6, WIT 7, WIL 5, WYR 2
**Skills:** Axes 2, Brawl 2, Bargain 3, Command 2, Forage 2,
Rune-lore 1, Weather-sense 2
**Trigger:** Incompetence that wastes silver or endangers the vulnerable.
**Agenda:** Keep the band alive long enough to see one "good winter."
**Loyalty:** 4 (to the band's survival)

### Kell "Hook" -- Line Fighter

Broad shoulders, missing two fingers on left hand. Quiet intensity.
**Stats:** MIG 7, NIM 5, TOU 6, WIT 4, WIL 4, WYR 1
**Skills:** Axes 3, Shields 2, Brawl 2, Forage 1
**Trigger:** Being treated as disposable muscle.
**Agenda:** Carve out a small piece of land to die on without owing
anyone.
**Loyalty:** 3

### Thorne "Ash-Born" -- Skirmisher

Burn scars across half his face from a hall fire. Intense eyes, dark
hair. Territorial and grudge-holding.
**Stats:** MIG 6, NIM 6, TOU 5, WIT 5, WIL 5, WYR 1
**Skills:** Blades 2, Brawl 1, Track 3, Forage 2, Shelter 2,
Intimidate 1
**Trigger:** Harm to kin or shield-siblings.
**Agenda:** Settle the blood-debt with the rival clan that burned his
family hall.
**Loyalty:** 3

### Petra "Shepherd" -- Skirmisher (Shield-Maiden)

Tall, pale-haired, moves like she is herding dangerous livestock. Manages
the men with firm patience.
**Stats:** MIG 5, NIM 6, TOU 5, WIT 6, WIL 6, WYR 2
**Skills:** Spears 2, Shields 2, Command 2, Persuade 2, Heal 2,
Wyrd-reading 1
**Trigger:** Being given responsibility without authority.
**Agenda:** Discover whether her younger sister, taken by raiders two
winters ago, is still alive.
**Loyalty:** 4

### Ubbe "Ironside" -- Veteran Warrior

Lean and scarred. Missing two fingers on his left hand. Hard eyes that
measure everything. Speaks only when necessary.
**Stats:** MIG 5, NIM 4, TOU 6, WIT 5, WIL 4, WYR 1
**Skills:** Axes 3, Shields 2, Forage 2, Survival 2, Intimidate 1
**Trigger:** Hunger or pointless hardship.
**Agenda:** Save enough silver to disappear. Tired of bleeding for other
men's ambitions.
**Loyalty:** 2

## 3. Named Men and Commons

- Named Men treat commons with flat contempt mixed with pragmatic
  tolerance. Commons carry extra weight and take the worst watch shifts.
- If a common's clumsiness costs the band (slows a march, drops a pack,
  makes noise near a barrow), a Named Man may confront them -- a shove,
  a low warning, or denial of food.
- Triggers and Agendas can intersect with any member: a Named Man whose
  Trigger is "being slowed by the weak" grows colder toward the weak
  link. One whose Agenda involves protecting the vulnerable may show
  rare, grudging mercy.
- Earning respect is possible but slow. Consistent useful work, surviving
  danger without complaint, or discovering information through luck or
  low cunning can shift attitudes over time.

---

## 4. Mechanics for Named Men

- **Loyalty Check:** When a Trigger fires, captain makes Command check
  vs difficulty (see 20_SIMULATION_RULES.md). Failure drops Loyalty by 1.
- **Agenda Progress:** Moving toward a Named Man's Agenda = +1 Loyalty.
  Ignoring it for 30+ days = -1 Loyalty per month.
- **Call-Name Assignment:** Earned only through witnessed deeds. The band
  decides the name, not the man.
