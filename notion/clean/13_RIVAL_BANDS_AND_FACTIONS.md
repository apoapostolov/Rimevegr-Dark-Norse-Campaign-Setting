# Rival Bands and Factions

**Project:** Iron Ledger -- Mercenaries of the Rimevegr
**Purpose:** Rival Svarthird bands, jarls, and factions with updatable profiles. All stats use the Iron Ledger system.

---

## 1. Rival Svarthird Bands

### The Three Wolves (Tyrant Hird)

**Size:** Company (22 fighters)
**Leader:** Hadric the Younger ("Red Jaw") -- brutal, ambitious, collecting trophies from defeated foes.
**Reputation:** Feared for burning villages that refuse tribute.
**Current Activity:** Occupying Feldwick for the second time this year.
**Strengths:** Heavy melee, excellent intimidation.
**Weaknesses:** Internal loyalty fragile; Named Men plotting exits.

**Named Men:**

- **Varis** (First Blade) -- MIG 7, NIM 5, TOU 6, WIT 6, WIL 5, WYR 1. Calculating, secretly building an exit plan.
- **Crane** (Sergeant) -- MIG 8, NIM 4, TOU 7, WIT 3, WIL 6, WYR 1. Eldest Hadric brother, berserker-prone.
- **"The Lamp" Merric** -- MIG 5, NIM 7, TOU 5, WIT 7, WIL 5, WYR 1. Former executioner, cold and precise.

**Relation to Voss's Black Axes:** Cold hostility.

### The Bone Pack (Kin-Clan)

**Size:** Warband (11 fighters)
**Leader:** Pack-Elder Sura -- old, scarred, deeply traditional.
**Reputation:** Never abandon shield-siblings. Deadly at night.
**Current Activity:** Negotiating winter shelter near ancestral land.
**Strengths:** Extremely loyal, deadly in forests and at night.
**Weaknesses:** Blood obligations pull members away.

**Named Men:**

- **Fang** (Skirmisher) -- MIG 6, NIM 8, TOU 5, WIT 5, WIL 4, WYR 2. Lightning-fast scout with a bounty on his head.
- **"The Questioner" Hast** -- MIG 5, NIM 5, TOU 5, WIT 7, WIL 6, WYR 3. Asks dangerous questions. Surprisingly high Wyrd.

**Relation to Voss's Black Axes:** Neutral, blood-oath alliance possible.

### The Silent Oar (Military Hird)

**Size:** Warband (18 fighters)
**Leader:** Duve "Coldhand" Orn -- former officer, disciplined.
**Reputation:** Professional, moves like ghosts.
**Current Activity:** Preparing siege on a bandit-held ruin.
**Strengths:** Formation discipline, scouting.
**Weaknesses:** Mourning recent losses; morale dips in static defense.

**Named Men:**

- **"Boot" Pask** (Sergeant) -- MIG 8, NIM 4, TOU 7, WIT 4, WIL 5, WYR 1. Heavy brute who leads charges.
- **Ked "the Nail" Sovere** -- MIG 5, NIM 5, TOU 5, WIT 8, WIL 5, WYR 1. Obsessed with accountability.

**Relation to Voss's Black Axes:** Professional respect mixed with rivalry.

### The Hollow Hall (Rural Mob)

**Size:** Skirmishers (9 fighters)
**Leader:** Brek Saltmarch -- desperate, stubborn.
**Reputation:** Small, desperate, will take any job.
**Current Activity:** Struggling through a pest-clearance contract.
**Strengths:** Survive on very little; good foragers.
**Weaknesses:** Low morale, poor equipment, arguments.

**Named Men:**

- **Torve** (Shieldman) -- MIG 6, NIM 5, TOU 6, WIT 4, WIL 5, WYR 1. Brek's cousin. Refuses to leave even when the others talk about splitting. Fights with a cracked wooden shield he will not replace.
- **"Mirke One-Ear"** (Scout) -- MIG 5, NIM 7, TOU 4, WIT 5, WIL 4, WYR 2. Lost his right ear to frostbite two winters ago. Good tracker but prone to wandering off when the Hush falls.

**Relation to Voss's Black Axes:** Ignored unless competing for same work.

### The Iron Tide Remnant (Military Hird)

**Size:** Warband (14 fighters)
**Leader:** Sigrid Blackwine -- ice-calm, pragmatic. Took command after the old captain froze to death on a bad march.
**Reputation:** Disciplined survivors of a once-great company that numbered sixty. The name still carries weight even if the men do not.
**Current Activity:** Wintering in Raven's Perch, rebuilding strength.
**Strengths:** Hard veterans who have survived everything. Excellent morale in adversity because the worst has already happened.
**Weaknesses:** No one in the band knows rune-lore; they avoid barrows and supernatural contracts, which limits their contract pool.

**Named Men:**

- **Sigrid Blackwine** (Captain) -- MIG 6, NIM 5, TOU 7, WIT 7, WIL 7, WYR 1. Skills: Axes 2, Shields 3, Command 3, Intimidate 2, Navigate 1, Bargain 2, Sagas 2. Armor: Chainmail shirt (torso 5, arms 3), iron helm (head 4). Weapons: Iron axe (base 6), seax (base 4). Trigger: Orders that waste hard-bought men for a jarl's pride. Agenda: Rebuild the Iron Tide to thirty fighters and reclaim their old winter hall in the south.
- **Halvard "Keg"** (Sergeant) -- MIG 7, NIM 5, TOU 7, WIT 5, WIL 6, WYR 1. Skills: Axes 2, Brawl 3, Smithing 2, Command 1, Shelter 1. Armor: Padded gambeson (torso 3, arms 2). Weapons: Long axe (base 9, two-hand). Trigger: Captain acting like the old captain who got them killed. Agenda: Forge a sword worthy of his dead father's name.
- **Ylva "the Mute"** (Scout) -- MIG 5, NIM 8, TOU 5, WIT 6, WIL 5, WYR 2. Skills: Track 3, Bows 2, Navigate 2, Forage 1, Stealth 2. Armor: Thick leather (torso 2, arms 1, legs 1). Weapons: Short bow (base 5), seax (base 4). Trigger: Being forced to enter barrows or touch rune-stones. Agenda: Find the body of her brother, lost on the march that broke the old Iron Tide, and give him proper rites. Note: Does not speak. Communicates by gesture, expression, and occasionally carving single words into tree bark.

**Relation to Voss's Black Axes:** Professional respect. Open to temporary alliance against the Three Wolves if approached.

### The Grave Wardens (Kin-Clan)

**Size:** Skirmishers (7 fighters)
**Leader:** Elder Bryn Ashmouth -- gaunt, devout, missing three fingers on his left hand from a barrow-curse.
**Reputation:** Specialists. They clear barrows or die trying. Other bands think them half-mad. Jarls pay them well because no one else will do the work.
**Current Activity:** Camped near the Iron Barrow south of Grimholt, preparing to breach an old hall where three previous attempts failed.
**Strengths:** Two rune-lore practitioners in a band of seven. Unmatched barrow-clearing experience. Carry galdr wards that the dead respect.
**Weaknesses:** Too small for open-field fights. Religious zealots who may refuse contracts that conflict with their interpretation of the old ways. Difficult to work with.

**Named Men:**

- **Elder Bryn Ashmouth** (Captain) -- MIG 5, NIM 4, TOU 5, WIT 6, WIL 8, WYR 3. Skills: Spears 1, Rune-lore 2, Spirit-lore 1, Wyrd-reading 2, Command 2, Sagas 3, Heal 1. Armor: Padded gambeson (torso 3, arms 2). Weapons: Spear (base 6), ritual knife (base 4). Trigger: Desecration of old graves or disrespect to the dead. Agenda: Seal the Iron Barrow permanently and earn the jarls' recognition of the Grave Wardens as a sanctioned order, not a mercenary band.
- **Sev "the Pale"** (Galdr-Speaker) -- MIG 4, NIM 5, TOU 4, WIT 6, WIL 7, WYR 4. Skills: Rune-lore 3, Wyrd-reading 2, Spirit-lore 1, Heal 2. Armor: None (rune-inscribed wool tunic). Weapons: Ritual knife (base 4). Trigger: Being asked to use galdr for profit rather than necessity. Agenda: Decode the rune-language spoken by the oldest barrow draugr, which he believes contains a warning about the Veil. Note: Covered in rune tattoos from neck to wrists. Skin is corpse-pale, as though the galdr has drained something vital from him.
- **Norn** (Grave-Guard) -- MIG 8, NIM 4, TOU 8, WIT 3, WIL 6, WYR 1. Skills: Axes 3, Shields 2, Brawl 2. Armor: Chainmail hauberk (torso 5, arms 3, legs 3), iron helm (head 4). Weapons: Long axe (base 9, two-hand). Trigger: Any order to retreat from a barrow before the dead are put down. Agenda: Kill the draugr lord of the Iron Barrow, which killed his sworn oathmate two summers ago. Note: Largest man most have ever seen. Does not speak much but when he does, the other Wardens stop and listen.

**Relation to Voss's Black Axes:** Neutral. Could become allies if the band needs barrow-clearing expertise or if Voss's blood-debt arc intersects with barrow lore. Hostile if the band desecrates graves.

### The Red Tide (Tyrant Hird)

**Size:** Company (20 fighters)
**Leader:** Wulfgar the Branded -- face branded on both cheeks with a thrall's mark that he wears as a challenge. Former thrall who killed his owner, took the man's sword, and built a band from the desperate and the cruel.
**Reputation:** Slavers and reavers. No settlement trusts them. They take thralls as primary payment and sell them south. Every jarl wants them gone but none can afford the cost of destroying them.
**Current Activity:** Roaming the Northern Ice-Reach and coastline, raiding isolated hamlets and fishing boats.
**Strengths:** Fast raiders who strike without warning. Ruthless economy based on thrall-taking. Wulfgar is a terrifying fighter and a cunning planner despite his crude reputation.
**Weaknesses:** Universally hated. No settlement will give them winter harbor or fair trade. Their own men fear Wulfgar more than they respect him, which means a single bad loss could shatter the band.

**Named Men:**

- **Wulfgar the Branded** (Captain) -- MIG 8, NIM 6, TOU 7, WIT 6, WIL 8, WYR 1. Skills: Blades 3, Brawl 2, Intimidate 3, Command 2, Navigate 1, Track 1, Deceive 2. Armor: Chainmail shirt (torso 5, arms 3), iron helm (head 4). Weapons: Sword (base 7), seax (base 4). Trigger: Anyone calling him thrall or implying he is still property. Agenda: Burn the hall of the jarl who enslaved him and take the man's children as thralls. Then retire south with enough silver to buy land.
- **Askel "Rope"** (Slaver) -- MIG 6, NIM 6, TOU 5, WIT 7, WIL 4, WYR 1. Skills: Blades 2, Deceive 2, Bargain 3, Navigate 2, Track 1. Armor: Thick leather (torso 2, arms 1, legs 1). Weapons: Sword (base 7), rope-garrote (base 3, grapple). Trigger: Losing a valuable thrall to escape or rescue. Agenda: Accumulate 500 silver in thrall sales and disappear to a southern coast. Note: Plans raids with a merchant's precision. Keeps a separate ledger tracking thrall inventory and market values.
- **Bera "Iron-Teeth"** (Champion) -- MIG 8, NIM 5, TOU 7, WIT 4, WIL 7, WYR 1. Skills: Axes 3, Shields 2, Brawl 3, Intimidate 2. Armor: Chainmail hauberk (torso 5, arms 3, legs 3), iron helm (head 4). Weapons: Long axe (base 9, two-hand). Trigger: Any suggestion she is less dangerous than the men. Agenda: Kill a rival champion in single combat witnessed by at least twenty men. Note: Files her teeth to points. The effect is purely psychological but effective. Most opponents hesitate when she grins.

**Relation to Voss's Black Axes:** Hostile. The Red Tide raids the same coastline settlements that contract with Voss's band. A confrontation is inevitable if arcs overlap. Wulfgar might offer a temporary non-aggression pact if both bands face a common enemy (Ordovast's Host), but he would betray it the moment the threat passes.

## 2. Major Jarls and Warchiefs

### Jarl Hrothgar of Frostfjord

- **Power Base:** Frostfjord Hollow (Village)
- **Personality:** Calculating, greedy, keeps his word when profitable.
- **Agenda:** Consolidate control over the Inner Fjords.
- **Relation:** Pays on time but demands exclusivity.

### Warchief Ordovast the Iron-Grip

- **Power Base:** Grimholt (Large Village, High Rime-Moors)
- **Personality:** Brutal, expansionist.
- **Agenda:** Recruit or crush smaller bands into a Host.
- **Danger Level:** High. Offers Sworn allegiance but demands total loyalty.

### The Pale Widow (Jarl-Elect of Ashen Reach)

- **Power Base:** Ashen Reach (Large Village)
- **Personality:** Cold, intelligent, recently widowed.
- **Agenda:** Remove troublesome Svarthird bands without paying them.
- **Method:** Subtle intrigue and hidden bounties.

## 3. Additional Jarls (Expansion Settlements)

### Jarl Sigrun of Deepholm

- **Power Base:** Deepholm (Small Town, Mountain)
- **Personality:** Pragmatic, wealthy, values professionalism.
- **Agenda:** Maintain Deepholm's independence from Ordovast's expansion.
- **Method:** Hires the best bands at premium rates. Keeps a galdr-speaker on retainer. Rival to Ordovast but prefers cold economics to open war.

### Jarl Ulf Vargson of Vargheim

- **Power Base:** Vargheim (Large Village, Forest)
- **Personality:** Traditional, wolf-obsessed, suspicious of outsiders.
- **Agenda:** Protect his wolf-warden kennels and the charcoal trade.
- **Method:** Pays fair but expects bands to respect his wolves and his territory. Wolf-pack raids on his livestock are his primary concern.

### Jarl Helga of Thornwall

- **Power Base:** Thornwall (Large Village, Moors)
- **Personality:** Shrewd, protective of her people, hard bargainer.
- **Agenda:** Protect sheep routes from the Red Tide and moor raiders.
- **Method:** Hires bands for escort and patrol. Pays in wool and mutton as often as silver.
